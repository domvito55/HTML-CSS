Dell Inspiron 14" (5410)

Do you need a new computer for either accessing the Internet, writing documents, or playing silly games? Consider buying one of the many versions of the Dell Inspiron 14".

Versions

If you just need a personal computer for day-to-day use, the version in conventional laptop format with Intel® Core™ i3 should be enough for you. If you are a student, you may consider buying a 2-in-1 format so you can use it as a computer and take class notes using a stylus. You can save some money buying the version with Core™ i5, 8 GB of RAM, and 256 GB SSD. But with only $50.00 more, you can buy a much more powerful machine with i7, 12 GB of RAM, and 512 GB SSD.

Features

  • Windows 11
  • 3 USB ports and 1 HDMI Port
  • Intagrated 720p camera
  • Intagrated Wi-fi, and Bluetooth 5.1
